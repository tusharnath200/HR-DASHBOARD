# HR-DASHBOARD
This project presents an interactive HR Dashboard designed to analyze employee data and provide actionable insights for decision-making. The dashboard helps HR teams understand workforce distribution, salary trends, and departmental performance.

# Key Insights and Features
Key Insights

👥 Workforce Distribution

Majority of employees belong to the 26–35 age group, indicating a young and active workforce.

Very few employees fall in the 55+ category, showing low senior-age representation.

💰 Salary Trends

There is a significant gap between highest and lowest salaries, highlighting pay disparity.

Top earners receive almost 2x–3x higher salary than bottom earners.

🎓 Education vs Income

Employees with higher education (e.g., Life Sciences, Medical) tend to have higher average income.

Lower education categories show comparatively reduced salary distribution.

🏢 Department Analysis

Technical and Sales departments contribute heavily to overall salary expenditure.

HR department shows a more balanced and moderate salary structure.
